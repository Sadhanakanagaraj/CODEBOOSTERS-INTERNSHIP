• What is an accordian panel?

    The Accordion Panel component is used to hold content within a panel that can be expanded or collapsed. This is ideal for content that does not need to be exposed all at once and is useful for saving space on a page.

  • What is the difference between accordian and dropdown?

    The accordion will push down the content, while the dropdown lays over the content.

  • What is the accordian toggle menu?

    Accordion menu can have a separate submenu toggle. This allows the parent item to have its own link, but still allows the submenu to be opened/closed.
